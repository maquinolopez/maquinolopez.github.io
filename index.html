About Me

I'm a researcher in statistics originally from Oaxaca, Mexico. I specialize in Bayesian methods with applications in paleoclimatology, age-depth modeling, and uncertainty quantification. I am currently an Associate Researcher at CIMAT, Mexico, and have previously worked at the University of Cambridge, Queen’s University Belfast, and other institutions.
